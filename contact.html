Prospective Postdoc Fellows:

Interested applicants should submit a CV and a cover letter outlining prior research experience, proposed project directions, and long-term career goals to t.sun [at] northeastern.edu.

Prospective PhD Students:

We welcome applications from prospective PhD students. Candidates must apply through the PhD programs offered with the Bioengineering, and/or Electrical & Computer Engineering Departments:

  • PhD in Bioengineering - https://bioe.northeastern.edu/academics/graduate-studies/phd-bion/
  • PhD in Electrical Engineering - https://ece.northeastern.edu/academics/graduate-studies/phd-elee/
  • PhD in Interdisciplinary Engineering - https://coe.northeastern.edu/academic-programs/phd-inte/

In addition, applicants are encouraged to email a CV, transcripts (unofficial or official), and a cover letter summarizing prior research and educational experience, along with a brief statement of career goals, to t.sun [at] northeastern.edu.
